Artist Statement

The work lives between restraint and release—like a held breath becoming a prayer.

Process

I build with a single continuous line and let color arrive like weather—soft, imperfect, and honest. The emptiness is intentional: space becomes a witness.

“I want the work to feel like being carried through the dark.”

Meaning

These pieces sit inside the tension of pain and hope. Some are abstract—pure emotion and rhythm. Others are figurative—faith rendered quietly, without spectacle.

“Found, not famous. Held, not performed.”
